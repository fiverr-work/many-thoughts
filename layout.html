FALL OF THE LEAVES

story and photos by Daniel Vineyard

Touching the clouds that move across the Apline Loop near Midway, Utah in the fall of 2019. I walked onto the mountain top, Timpanogas, and saw the leaves fall at my feet. An Autumn day in Utah.
